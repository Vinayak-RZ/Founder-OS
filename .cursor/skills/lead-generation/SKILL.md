# Lead generation

## When to use
- Define ICP (industry, size, role, geography)
- Build prospect lists from public sources and CRM
- Prioritize who to contact and why now
- Draft first-touch outreach (approval-gated send)

## Workflow
1. Clarify ICP and offer (one sentence)
2. Source prospects (CRM + vault notes + web — cite sources)
3. Score: fit, timing, channel
4. Output table: name, company, role, angle, draft hook

## Founder OS role
Leads/Outreach specialists update CRM and drafts. Deep list building may happen in Cursor; store outputs in `leads/` vault folder.
